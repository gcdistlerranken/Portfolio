Meet Grace Distler



Grace is a fourth semester student at Ranken Technical College, studying Application and Web Development. She is proficient in HTML and CSS coding language, as well as experienced in JavaScript and jQuery. She is also skilled in ASP.NET MVC. She finished up her last semester at Ranken studying Java Android language. She is 21 years old, and has great interest in getting a job in the IT industry.

In early life, Grace went to elementary school at St. Joseph Manchester. She then went on to high school at Ursuline Academy. After graduating high school, she attended Missouri University of Science and Technology. For 3 semesters Grace studied a degree in Information Science and Technology. In January 2018, she attended St. Louis Community College at Wildwood. She did not finish the semester there but starting in August 2018, she began her education at Ranken Technical College, working towards her Associate's Degree in IT. She attended the Ranken St. Louis location for two semesters, and then transferred to the Ranken Wentzville location, where she finished her education.
